About Me | Jacqueline R. Hackett

Sept. 2018

Jacqueline Hackett (born January 14, 1999) is a singer-songwriter based in New York City with a folk/rock influence. Originally from Northern New Jersey, she has been singing and playing guitar since the age of 9. She is inspired by artists such as Jewel, Bob Dylan, Laura Marling, Eric Clapton, and many many more. She frequently plays shows in both NYC + Nashville and is excited to finally release her original music very soon.

Other passions include political activism (with an emphasis on gun control and LGBTQ+ rights), attempting to cook meals for her girlfriend, hiking, traveling, The Bachelor and filmmaking- a new found love. Currently studying at Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts at the New School as a second year student, Jacqueline is looking forward to all the opportunities ahead of her in the future!
